My Testimony (The Knowledge I Gained from this Opportunity )

This training on Empowering Community ALS Implementers gave me a lot of great experiences and valuable learnings that makes me a competitive teacher in terms of digital citizenship. This training is a big help especially that we are in the 21st century where technology use is a must. That summed up my learning experiences in my ICT training here at Northern Bukidnon State College. So grateful that I have this opportunity to be in this training since it is a big boost to my growth as a responsible and literate digital citizen. I am very greatful to be part of this training.
